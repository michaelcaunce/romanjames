Behind The Lens

Travel Photographer of the year 2019, Roman James is an internationally-renowned nature, wildlife, and travel photographer from London. His unrivalled passion for adventure has been the driving force behind his life’s quest to capture the moods and character of the world’s most amazing places, from Africa to the Amazon to the Arctic and countless places in between.

Roman was named one of the “Top 10 Influential Photographers on the Web” by the Empire Post with recent features including DSCVR, Travellers, Roam and many more. Roman is a true global influencer in the fields of photography, travel, and wildlife conservation with more than 3 million social media followers.
